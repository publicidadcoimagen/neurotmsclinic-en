Frequently asked questions

General answers to help you understand the process. Clinical evaluation and personalized recommendations happen during consultation.

Does TMS hurt?
TMS is typically well tolerated. Some people notice a mild scalp sensation during stimulation. We address questions during the initial assessment.
Does it require sedation or anesthesia?
It typically does not require sedation. Sessions are outpatient.
How long is a session?
Session time may vary depending on the protocol. We provide details after the initial assessment.
How many sessions are recommended?
It depends on the individual plan. Often, sessions are scheduled across multiple weeks.
Who may be a candidate?
TMS is considered in different contexts depending on professional evaluation. Eligibility is determined during consultation.
Can I schedule via WhatsApp?
We may contact you via WhatsApp for informational and administrative purposes, with your consent.
