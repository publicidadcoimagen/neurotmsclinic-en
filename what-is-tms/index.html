What is Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS)?

TMS is a non-invasive neuromodulation technique that uses magnetic stimulation to target specific brain areas. The materials describe it as supported by scientific research and used in different clinical contexts:contentReference[oaicite:11]{index=11}.
